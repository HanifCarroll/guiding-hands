Mission

Guiding Hands promotes and encourages the participation of the participants in taking an active role in making decisions about their funds.

Guiding Hands is not just a financial organization, but also offers guidance that will be beneficial to the participants.

Goals

Organize finances according to the wishes of the participants.

Provide on time payments and account statements are available upon request.

Provide the best advice that will be beneficial to participants.

Treat each person with dignity and respect.

Who Needs Payee Services?

  • Elderly
  • Minors
  • Intellectually Disabled
  • Mentally Challenged
  • Adults in Crisis
  • Anyone needing financial management
